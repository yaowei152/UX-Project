DISCIPLINES

In NYP Atheletics, we have 3 different disciplines which includes Sprints, Jumps, Throws, and Distance

|

Sprints

Running short distances as fast as possible.

Competetive races where speed meets endurance, dash down the track in events such as 100m, 200m, 400m, and hurdles!

Jumps

Achieve the greatest height or distance.

It's a test for athleticism! Leap for height and distance in events like Long Jump, Triple Jump, High Jump, and Pole Vault!

Distance

Test of endurance on the track.

Be physically and mentally strong through distance running. Run distances from 800m to as long as 10,000m!

Throws

Throw as far as you can!

Where atheletes aim to throw an object as far as possible using Strength, Speed, Technique, and Coordination.

FAQ

  • Q1: What disciplines are offered in NYP Athletics?

    NYP Athletics offers four main disciplines:

    - Sprints: 100m, 200m, 400m, hurdles

    - Jumps: Long Jump, Triple Jump, High Jump, Pole Vault

    - Distance: 800m to 10,000m races

    - Throws: Discus, Javelin, Shotput

  • Q2: When and where are training sessions held?

    Training schedules vary by discipline. Most sessions are held at the NYP track or nearby sports facilities. You'll receive a detailed schedule upon joining.

  • Q3: Who can join NYP Athletics?

    Any NYP student with a passion for athletics is welcome! Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, there's a place for you.

  • Q4: How do I sign up for the CCA?

    You can sign up through the NYP CCA portal or contact the Athletics team directly at athletics@nyp.edu.sg.
