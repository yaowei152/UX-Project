Our Journey of Excellence

2022

POL-ITE Championships

Our athletes brought home 5 gold, 3 silver, and 7 bronze medals, marking one of our most successful years in the Polytechnic-ITE (POL-ITE) games.

2023

IVP Games Victory

Secured the overall championship trophy at the Institute-Varsity-Polytechnic (IVP) Games, with standout performances in track and field events.

Early 2024

Community Outreach Program

Launched a new initiative to mentor young athletes from local secondary schools, sharing our passion for sports and promoting a healthy lifestyle.

Mid 2024

New Training Facilities

Unveiled our state-of-the-art training facility, including an all-weather running track and a fully equipped strength and conditioning gym.
